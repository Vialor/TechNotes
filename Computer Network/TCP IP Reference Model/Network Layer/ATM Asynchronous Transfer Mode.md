- a high-speed networking technology that is designed to transmit voice, video, and data over a wide range of distances
- uses a connection-oriented approach, in which a dedicated connection is established between two devices before data transmission begins
- not compatible with IP
# Structure
Cell: the data unit of ATM  
has fixed size: 5 bytes header + 48 bytes payload  
- ATM has two components:
    
    ATM switches: connected to an end-point or another switch
    
    ATM end-points
    
- ATM switches supports two interfaces:
    
    UNI User-Network Interface: between end-points and switches
    
    NNI Network-Network Interface: between two switches
    
- Three types of services
    
    Permanent Virtual Circuits PVC: connection established in advance
    
    Switched Virtual Circuits SVC (most used): connection established on demand
    
    Connectionless services
